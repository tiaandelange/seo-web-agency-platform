## Positioning

Primary: Koppie Systems builds SEO-first websites and practical digital systems for technical, industrial and service businesses.

Expanded: websites, ecommerce platforms, customer portals, RFQ systems and workflow tools that help established businesses attract enquiries and operate more efficiently.

Geographic: Based in Pretoria and works with businesses throughout South Africa.

## Core idea

Elevation through practical digital infrastructure. A koppie is stable, recognisable and grounded — it rises without pretending to be a mountain. Metaphor: visibility, organisation, effective operation, stable foundation, practical upward commercial movement.

Must feel: technically competent, commercially focused, distinctly South African, approachable, modern without trend-chasing, credible for industrial/engineering/professional clients.

Must not look: rustic, agricultural, outdoorsy, tourism, childish, engineering-consultancy, construction, generic startup, cheap hosting.

## Personality

Grounded · Structured · Capable · Approachable · Purposeful · Reliable · South African

## Voice

Clear, confident, technically informed, commercially realistic, direct, useful. Lead with outcomes. Be specific. Never use ranking/growth guarantees or unsupported superlatives.

## Messaging pillars

1. Visibility — found and understood.
2. Conversion — visitors take action.
3. Operations — less repetitive admin.
4. Reliability — secure, maintained, useful after launch.
