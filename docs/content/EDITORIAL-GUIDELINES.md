# Editorial guidelines

## Voice

Engineering-led, plain, confident, specific. We explain; we don't hype. Written like a competent professional talking to a business owner across a table.

- en-ZA spelling (optimise, colour, enquiry, metre); ZAR amounts as "R25,000".
- Short sentences. Active voice. No agency clichés ("cutting-edge", "world-class", "unleash", "elevate your brand").
- Numbers and specifics beat adjectives ("pages load in under 2.5 seconds on mobile" not "blazing fast") — but only real numbers.
- Address the reader as "you"; refer to us as "we".

## Honesty rules (non-negotiable, from blueprint rules 10–13)

1. Never promise rankings, traffic, or timeframes-to-rank. Approved phrasing: "built to meet the technical and content standards Google rewards" / "no honest provider can guarantee rankings".
2. No invented clients, reviews, statistics, awards, team sizes, or years of experience.
3. Placeholders (pricing, bios, case-study templates) are visibly labelled as indicative/example.
4. If we cite market figures, we date them and name the source.
5. New-studio honesty: we say we're new when relevant and convert it into a strength (senior attention, modern stack, documented method).

## Structure conventions

- H1 states the topic; first two sentences answer the page's core question; no warm-up paragraphs.
- H2s are scannable claims or questions, not labels ("What a catalogue website includes", not "Features").
- Bullets for scope lists; prose for reasoning; tables for comparisons.
- Every page ends with one clear next step (its designated CTA), never three competing ones.
- FAQ answers: 2–5 sentences, self-contained, no "see above".

## SEO-specific writing rules

- Primary phrase in H1 + intro + one H2, naturally — if it reads forced, rewrite the sentence, don't force the phrase.
- Cover the intent's sub-questions on the same page (that's what keeps variations consolidated).
- Anchor text descriptive; links inside sentences where they carry meaning.
- Don't restate numbers that live on /pricing/ inside other pages — link instead (single source prevents stale copies).

## Review gate before `live`

Author self-check against ON-PAGE-SEO-CHECKLIST → factual pass (every claim true? every placeholder labelled?) → read-aloud pass (does it sound like a person?) → validator green.
