## On-site local signals (implemented)

- `/areas-we-serve/` hub + genuinely distinct Pretoria and Johannesburg pages (unique copy rule 11; Centurion/Sandton consolidated, stated on-page).
- `ProfessionalService` schema with `areaServed`; no fake address fields.
- Locale `en_ZA`, ZAR pricing context, SA-specific content (POPIA, PayFast/Yoco references) — natural relevance signals.
- Contact page states service model plainly (remote-first, on-site meetings in Gauteng — confirm wording).

## Citation strategy (post-naming only)

Order: Google Business Profile → LinkedIn company page → Clutch/TechBehemoths/GoodFirms/DesignRush listings (they own the "top company" SERPs — see competitor research) → local SA directories (Brabys, Cylex, Snupit — evaluate quality first). Identical NAP everywhere, tracked in a simple sheet.

## Review approach

Ask every completed-project client for a Google review via a direct GBP review link, one follow-up maximum, never incentivised, never gated (filtering happy customers = policy violation). Respond to every review. Reviews appear on the website only with permission and only verbatim.
