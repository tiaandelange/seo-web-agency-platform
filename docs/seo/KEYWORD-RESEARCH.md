# Keyword research

Date: 2026-07-21. No paid keyword tool was available; demand ratings are **qualitative** (High/Med/Low) inferred from SERP density, competitor investment and market logic (A-07). Re-score with a keyword tool + GSC data after launch. One page per material intent — variations consolidate (rule: no page merely because a phrase exists).

## Cluster inventory

### 1. Generic commercial (High demand, High competition)
`website design south africa`, `web design company south africa`, `website developers south africa`, `web development company`, `website design near me`.
→ Destination: **Home** (+ `/services/` index). Long-game cluster; won via overall authority, not a single page.

### 2. Local commercial (High, Med–High)
`website design pretoria`, `web development pretoria`, `website designers johannesburg`, `web design gauteng`, `website design centurion`.
→ `/areas-we-serve/pretoria/`, `/areas-we-serve/johannesburg/` (Centurion/Gauteng consolidated into Pretoria page — no separate pages, D-08).

### 3. Price/packages (High, Med) — highest conversion intent
`website design prices south africa`, `website cost south africa`, `how much does a website cost`, `business website packages south africa`, `affordable website design` (we deliberately do not chase “cheap website design”).
→ `/pricing/` (commercial) + `/resources/website-cost-south-africa/` (informational) + `/website-packages/` (transactional). Three intents, three pages, heavily cross-linked; cannibalisation notes in the CSV.

### 4. Service-specific commercial (Med, Med)
Per service: `business website design`, `lead generation website`, `product catalogue website south africa`, `ecommerce website development south africa`, `website redesign south africa`, `seo website design south africa`, `website maintenance south africa` (+ `website maintenance cost`).
→ The 11 `/services/…` pages. Each owns its cluster + question variants answered in on-page FAQs.

### 5. Systems/custom development (Low–Med, Low) — differentiation wedge
`custom web application development south africa`, `admin panel development`, `quotation system development`, `rfq system south africa`, `customer portal development`, `supplier portal`, `business process automation small business`, `next.js development south africa`.
→ The 4 custom-system service pages. `next.js development` consolidates into `/services/custom-web-applications/` + `/compare/wordpress-vs-nextjs/` until demand justifies a dedicated page (noted in CSV).

### 6. Industry × website (Low–Med, Low) — topical-authority builders
`websites for contractors`, `construction company website design`, `engineering company website`, `manufacturer website design`, `website for law firm south africa`, `real estate / property website development`.
→ The 6 `/solutions/…` pages.

### 7. Comparison/decision (Med, Low–Med)
`wordpress vs custom website`, `wordpress vs next.js`, `website builder vs web developer`, `template vs custom website`, `website vs web application`, `website maintenance options / do i need website maintenance`.
→ The 4 `/compare/…` pages (builder-vs-developer consolidates into custom-vs-template).

### 8. Question/informational long-tail (Med, Low)
`how long does a website take to build`, `what is seo website design`, `how to choose a web developer`, `do i need a new website or redesign`, `website not showing on google`.
→ `/resources/` articles per the 12-month roadmap; each supports a named commercial page.

## Prioritisation logic

P1 (launch): clusters 3, 4-core (business/lead-gen/ecommerce/maintenance/seo-dev), 2-Pretoria, 7 (two pages).
P2 (months 1–3): remaining services, solutions, Johannesburg, remaining compares, first articles.
P3 (months 3–12): article long-tail, remaining solutions depth, Cape Town when justified.
