# Support package decision

Prepared 2026-07-21 for owner sign-off. Reviews the seven plans in `SUPPORT-PACKAGES.md` for launch practicality (solo founder capacity) and commercial logic. All amounts remain indicative until confirmed (input #6).

## Recommendation: launch with three plans, not seven

Seven plans are architecturally sound but operationally heavy for a solo founder and cognitively heavy for buyers. Consolidate the *presentation* to three launch plans plus two add-ons; keep the page structure unchanged (one URL, `/services/website-maintenance-and-support/`).

### Launch plans

| Plan | Merges | Indicative /month | For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Essential care** | Hosting administration + Essential website care | R850–R1,500 | Starter builds and any site we manage: hosting/domain/SSL/DNS admin, updates, backups, monitoring, 30 min small edits, monthly health check |
| **Business support** | Business website support (as is) | R1,800–R3,500 | Lead-gen/professional sites: Essential + 2h changes, form/lead monitoring, Search Console review, quarterly improvement call |
| **System & store support** | Ecommerce support + Web application support | R3,000–R8,000 (quoted within band per system) | Online stores and custom systems: SLA response targets, payment/checkout or workflow monitoring, bug fixes, dependency updates, small-enhancement backlog |

### Add-ons (attach to any plan)

| Add-on | Indicative /month |
|---|---|
| Search performance reporting (GSC/GA plain-language report) | R950–R1,800 |
| Content & SEO support (1–2 mapped articles/pages + metadata/linking upkeep) | R4,500–R9,000 |

Rationale for merging: hosting-only clients are rare and low-margin at launch (fold into Essential); ecommerce and application support share the same operational reality (SLA, monitoring, updates) and differ only in what is monitored — one plan, quoted within the band, is simpler to sell and staff.

## Support boundaries (state on the page and in proposals)

- Response targets, not resolution guarantees; targets defined per plan (propose: Essential next business day; Business same business day; System/store 4 business hours) — **owner must confirm capacity before publishing**.
- Included hours don't roll over; out-of-plan work at the published hourly rate.
- Plans cover the site/system we built or formally onboarded (an onboarding audit is chargeable for sites built by others).
- Excluded: new features beyond the small-enhancement backlog, content writing beyond the add-on, third-party outages, paid-ads management.
- No plan promises rankings; reporting shows real Search Console data only.

## Commercial logic check

Ten Business-support clients ≈ R18k–R35k/month recurring (unchanged from `SUPPORT-PACKAGES.md`) — the cash-flow floor. Attach rule stands: every build proposal includes a recommended care plan by default.

## Owner decisions required

1. Approve the 3-plan + 2-add-on launch presentation (or keep all seven visible).
2. Confirm response-time targets you can actually meet solo.
3. Confirm cancellation terms (30 days) and billing method.
4. Confirm indicative monthly ranges before they lose the "indicative" label.
