## Screening rules applied

Excluded during generation: anything with hyphens; “SA Web…”, “…Hosting”, “Cheap/Quick/Easy…” (low-cost vibe); WordPress/Wix/tech-locked names; “RankFirst/PageOne”-type ranking implications; playful spellings (Webz, Kre8); names colliding with known SA agencies found in research (Gridweb, Weblab, Brave, Bluegrass, Ruby, Warp, Breeze, Woww).
