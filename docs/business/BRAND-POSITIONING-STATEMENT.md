# Brand positioning statement

Status: recommended 2026-07-21, pending owner sign-off (`docs/REQUIRED-USER-INPUTS.md` #15). Name-agnostic — written to survive the final naming decision. Evidence base: `POSITIONING-STRATEGY.md` (re-verified 2026-07-21), `docs/seo/COMPETITOR-RESEARCH.md`, `TARGET-CUSTOMERS.md`.

## The statement

> **For owners and directors of technical, industrial and service businesses in South Africa** who need their website to produce qualified enquiries — not just exist — **[Company] builds SEO-first websites and the business systems behind them**: lead-generation sites, product catalogues with RFQ workflows, ecommerce, and the custom admin, quotation and portal systems that reduce the administration those businesses drown in. **Unlike volume template designers and gloss-first agencies**, the engineer who scopes the project builds it, every page exists because a documented search intent justifies it, and the same partner can extend the website into the systems that run the business.

## The six answers

### 1. Who the company serves

Owners and directors of contractors and trade businesses, engineering and industrial services, manufacturers/suppliers/distributors, property businesses and professional practices — South African SMEs with high job values whose buyers genuinely search Google before buying. Full profiles: `TARGET-CUSTOMERS.md`.

### 2. What it builds

SEO-first business and lead-generation websites; product catalogue and RFQ websites; ecommerce; website redesigns; and the expansion layer — custom admin panels, quotation/RFQ systems, customer and supplier portals, workflow systems — plus maintenance and support that keeps all of it fast, secure and measured.

### 3. What business problem it solves

"The phone doesn't ring, and the admin eats our week." Concretely: invisibility in commercial searches, websites that describe rather than convert, technical products presented so badly buyers can't request a quote, and quotation/admin processes running on spreadsheets and email.

### 4. Why the company is different

- The person who scopes the work builds it — engineering-led, no account-manager relay.
- Search demand decides the architecture before design; the keyword-to-page map is shown as proof of method.
- Modern server-rendered stack (Next.js), not a page-builder theme — measurable Core Web Vitals headroom.
- Websites that grow into systems: catalogue → RFQ workflow → supplier portal, from one accountable partner.
- Clients own everything: code, content, domain, analytics. No builder lock-in, no hostage hosting.
- Straight published answers on cost and process in a "request a quote to see anything" market.

### 5. Why a client should trust it

Only verifiable claims: a documented, inspectable build method; a real engineering background; published process and indicative pricing; a demonstrably fast, accessible, structurally correct website of our own. No invented clients, results, ratings or years-in-business — trust assets grow per the proof roadmap in `DIFFERENTIATION.md` as permissioned case studies and testimonials land.

### 6. What the company does not compete on

- **Price.** Not the cheapest; does not answer R1,500–R8,000 template briefs (2026 market floor — see `POSITIONING-STRATEGY.md` evidence). The Starter package is the floor, not the pitch.
- **Volume.** No 50-sites-a-month production line.
- **Gloss.** Not an awards/brand-film agency; design serves conversion and credibility.
- **Guarantees.** Never sells rankings promises or "#1 on Google" (rule 10).
- **Everything-for-everyone.** No social-media retainers, paid-ads management or enterprise tenders at launch.

## One-line versions

- Spoken: "We build websites that get technical businesses found on Google and turn searches into quote requests — then we build the systems that handle the work coming in."
