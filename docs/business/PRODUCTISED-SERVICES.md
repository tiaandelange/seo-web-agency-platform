# Productised services

Scope definitions for every offering. All pricing figures are **indicative placeholders** (see `PRICING-ARCHITECTURE.md`; owner input #6). CTA abbreviations: RQ = Request a quote, BC = Book a consultation.

## Website services

### Starter business website
- Target: new/small businesses replacing no site or a DIY site. Problem: invisible to Google, no credibility.
- Scope: up to 6 pages (home, about, 2–3 services, contact), SEO-first structure, mobile-first, contact + WhatsApp, GSC/analytics setup.
- Exclusions: copywriting beyond structural guidance, logo design, ecommerce, custom features.
- CTA: RQ. Supports: Essential care plan. Indicative: R14,000–R25,000.

### Professional lead-generation website
- Target: contractors/service businesses needing enquiries. Problem: site doesn't rank or convert.
- Scope: 8–15 pages incl. service and area architecture from keyword mapping, conversion layout, quote forms, measurement events, case-study structure.
- Exclusions: ongoing SEO campaigns, paid ads management.
- CTA: RQ. Supports: Business support plan, content/SEO support. Indicative: R28,000–R60,000.

### Product catalogue website
- Target: manufacturers/suppliers with quotable product ranges. Problem: product info trapped in PDFs; no RFQ flow.
- Scope: structured product data model, category/product templates, search/filter (crawl-safe), spec sheets, RFQ basket → quote form.
- Exclusions: payments, live stock integration (scoped separately).
- CTA: RQ. Supports: catalogue updates via support plan; upgrade path to RFQ system. Indicative: R45,000–R90,000.

### Ecommerce website
- Target: retailers/B2B sellers taking payment. Problem: platform limits, poor SEO, high fees.
- Scope: product catalogue, cart/checkout with SA payment gateway (e.g. PayFast/Peach/Yoco — choose at scoping), delivery options, order emails, SEO product templates.
- Exclusions: ERP integration, marketplaces feeds (scoped separately).
- CTA: RQ. Supports: Ecommerce support plan. Indicative: R70,000–R160,000.

### Website redesign
- Target: owners of established but underperforming sites. Problem: outdated, slow, unrankable; fear of losing existing rankings.
- Scope: content/URL audit, redirect map, rebuilt architecture, migration with canonical/redirect control, before/after measurement.
- Exclusions: net-new content beyond restructure.
- CTA: BC (audit-first sale). Indicative: R30,000–R80,000.

### SEO website development
- Target: buyers who explicitly search “SEO web design”. Problem: past sites built pretty-first, found never.
- Scope: keyword-to-page mapping, architecture, technical SEO build, schema, GSC onboarding, 90-day indexation support.
- Exclusions: link building, ongoing rank campaigns.
- CTA: BC. Indicative: R35,000–R75,000.

## Custom systems

### Custom web applications
- Target: businesses whose process fits no product. Scope: discovery → spec → build (Next.js/TypeScript, hosted DB), auth, roles, reporting. Exclusions: fixed price before discovery. CTA: BC. Indicative: from R80,000; discovery R8,000–R15,000.

### Admin panel development
- Target: spreadsheet-run businesses. Scope: data model, CRUD screens, roles, exports, audit trail. CTA: BC. Indicative: R50,000–R150,000.

### RFQ and quotation systems
- Target: quote-driven sellers. Scope: enquiry intake, quote builder (line items, VAT), PDF output, status tracking, follow-up reminders. CTA: BC. Indicative: R60,000–R160,000.

### Customer and supplier portals
- Target: repeat-interaction B2B. Scope: login portal for orders/documents/statements/job status; supplier onboarding flows. CTA: BC. Indicative: R70,000–R200,000.

Related (sold under custom systems, no dedicated SEO page yet): property-management systems, bespoke workflow automation — case-study-led until search demand justifies pages.
